The Studio

Chaotic Play is a solo indie game studio created by Cassidy Noble. I have been making games off and on for the last 10 years giving them out as Christmas presents, participating in game jams, or making little projects for my own entertainment.

As I have progressed in my development, I've started to collect more and more ideas from games that I am inspired by (the Persona series, Rockstar, Stardew Valley, Animal Crossing, Sid Meier, and others) to create larger worlds and make them feel vibrant and lively.

The intent of this studio is to create games that are fun to play, but also a little out of the ordinary and stir some emotive response. I'm always open to working with people on various projects and the hope is this studio can start to grow to a small team to create passion projects together.
