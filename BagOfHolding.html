Bag of Holding

Get Now:

Download Bag of Holding

It is just a normal day in your normally quiet village of Arcton. You, Mohini, are sitting beneath the town's mythical Baobob tree. The tree drops a bag for you. Upon further instructions, the bag indicates, it can hold one and ONLY ONE item in it, but that item can be of any size.

Would you like to assist your grandmother with her groceries? Maybe the town's fountain needs to be moved? Perhaps the King would like to take a swim in the ocean? Maybe the new hotel location just isn't working for you.

Be careful though, the bag indicated that if you are not in good standing with your community, there will be consequences.

This game was submit for the GMTK 2019 theme - "Only One".
